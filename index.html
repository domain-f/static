— Our Story —

Born from Obsession

FUEGO started as a dream in a tiny kitchen in Chennai — Chef Arjun Mehta spending sleepless nights perfecting spice blends passed down through four generations.

Today, we serve over 300 guests a day, but every dish is still made with the same obsessive care. We source our spices directly from Chettinad, Coorg, and Kashmir.
